About the Project

The FlowState Productivity Tracker is designed to help individuals effectively manage their time, reduce distractions, and improve overall productivityt. In today's digital era, students, employees, and freelancers often struggle to stay focused due to the constant presence of social media, entertainment platforms, and other online distractions. This project addresses these challenges by providing a smart travking system that monitors how users spend their time, categorizes activities as productive or unproductive, and delivers detailed reports with actionable insights.

Through features such as real-time activity monitoring, goal-setting, notification, etc. the application empowers users to build better habits, achieve balance, and stay accountable to their priorities. Whether for academics purposes, professional tasks, or personal growth, FlowState offers a comprehensive tool for transforming time management into meaningful results. Ultimately, the project's mission is to guide users toward sustained focus, reduced procrastination, and long-term efficiency in both work and daily life.
